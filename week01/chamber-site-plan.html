Site Name

Chamber of Commerce

The site name was libson chamber of commerce, emphasizing its role in promoting business and community activities within the borough.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Chamber of Commerce website includes:

    • Our goal is to support and boost local businesses while attracting new investments.
    • We aim to create a platform that fosters community engagement, cultural events, business networking, and resource sharing for our members and the public.

Scenarios

questions from site visitors include:

  • Which chamber events are scheduled this month to facilitate business-to-business networking?
  • How can I access the contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What resources does the libson provide to support new businesses?
